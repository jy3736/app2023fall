Android Phone Screen Mirroring

To open the "Developer Options" and enable "USB Debugging" on your phone based on your settings:

"Follow these steps to access 'Developer Options' and enable 'USB Debugging' in your phone's settings:

  1. Unlock your phone and go to the home screen.
  1. Open the 'Settings' app.
  1. Scroll down and tap on 'System.'
  1. Find and tap on 'About Phone.'
  1. Look for the 'Build Number' entry and tap on it repeatedly (usually seven times) until you see a message confirming that you've unlocked the 'Developer Options.'
  1. Now, go back to the main 'Settings' menu, and you should see 'Developer Options' listed.
  1. Tap on 'Developer Options.'
  1. Scroll down to find 'USB Debugging' and toggle it on.

Your phone's 'USB Debugging' feature is now enabled, allowing you to use developer tools and connect your phone to a computer for debugging purposes."

To control the mirrored phone screen using a "mouse":

Here's how you can interact with the mirrored phone screen using a mouse:

  1. Ensure that your phone screen is being mirrored on your computer. You can use software like 'Scrcpy' for this purpose.
  1. Connect your computer's mouse to the USB port or use a touchpad if available.
  1. Move the mouse cursor on your computer screen to control the mouse pointer on the mirrored phone screen.
  1. Left-click: To tap or select an item on the phone screen, simply left-click (press and release) on it with your mouse cursor.
  1. Right-click: You can perform a right-click (usually by clicking the right mouse button) to access context menus or perform specific actions on items within the mirrored screen.
  1. Scrolling: If you have a scroll wheel on your mouse, you can use it to scroll through lists or webpages on the mirrored phone screen. Simply roll the scroll wheel up or down.
  1. Drag and Drop: To drag and drop items on the phone screen, click and hold the left mouse button on the item you want to move, drag it to the desired location, and release the mouse button to drop it.

This way, you can effectively control and interact with your phone's mirrored screen as if you were using a touchscreen.

Course Description:

Embark on a transformative journey in the "App Inventor Development and IoT Integration" course. Over 18 weeks, you'll master App Inventor for mobile app creation, explore JavaScript's power, and harness Node-RED for IoT innovation. Begin by grasping app essentials, UI design, and real-time Firebase integration. Craft a dice app and dive into an IoT Temperature Monitor simulation for your midterm project.

Expand your horizons with JavaScript game app development, and then delve into JavaScript basics. Unlock Node-RED's potential through flow design, logic integration, and IoT device incorporation. Craft a project proposal, ensuring your final project aligns with your vision. Progress discussions ensure you're on track before presenting your IoT solution in the course's culmination. Incorporate mobile apps, games, and IoT into your skill set. This course empowers you to innovate, equipping you with sought-after abilities in app development and IoT integration.
